How to make a Windmill?

Tools and Materials Needed:

  • Colored paper (square shape)
  • Glue dots
  • Scissors
  • Ruler
  • Pen
  • Paper straws
  • Pipe cleaners

Instructions:

  1. Choose a square piece of colored or patterned paper.
  2. Use a ruler to find and mark the center by drawing diagonals, corner to corner.
  3. Make a small hole in the center where the diagonals cross.
  4. Use scissors to cut along each diagonal, about halfway to the middle of the paper. Be sure not to cut all the way.
  5. You should now have 4 triangle flaps still connected at the center.
  6. Choose one corner of each triangle flap (always the left corner) and gently fold that corner towards the center.
  7. Hold each folded corner at the center with a glue dot, one at a time.
  8. After folding all 4 corners to the center, press them down with a glue dot.
  9. Use a pipe cleaner to poke through the center of the windmill.
  10. Then, wrap and twist the other end of the pipe cleaner around the top of the paper straw, leaving it a bit loose so the windmill can spin.
  11. Well done! You have made a windmill!
